❓ Lipotso Tse Di Bodiwang Gantsi

Lipotso tsotlhe tse di amanang le Porojeke ya Kago ya Ntlo ya Tirelo ya Tshenolo The Holy Church. Fa o sa bone karabo ya potso ya gago, tsweetswee ikgolaganye le Building Committee.

Building Project FAQ

Karolo eno e araba dipotso tse badumedi le baeng ba di botsang gantsi mabapi le porojeke ya kago.

🏛️ Goreng re aga Ntlo e Ntšhwa ya Tirelo?

Tshenolo The Holy Church e aga Ntlo e Ntšhwa ya Tirelo go dira bonnete jwa gore go nne le lefelo le le siametseng kobamelo, dithuto, dikopano le ditirelo tsotlhe tsa kereke.

Kago eno gape e tla solegela dikokomana tse di tlang molemo mme e nne boswa jwa kereke.

💰 Ke bokae jo bo tlhokegang go wetsa porojeke?

Budget yotlhe ya porojeke e bontshiwa mo go Progress Page. Fa madi a ntse a oketsega, tsotlhe di tla ntšhafadiwa gore badumedi ba bone tsotlhe ka nako tsotlhe.

📈 Re setse re kokoantse bokae?

Madi a a kokoantsweng a bontshiwa mo go Progress page mmogo le progress bar e e bontshang gore porojeke e setse e fitlhile kae.

🧱 A nka neela didirisiwa tsa kago?

Ee. Ga se madi fela a a amogelwang.

O ka neela:

  • 🧱 Ditena
  • 🪨 Samente
  • 🪟 Matlhabaphefo
  • 🚪 Mabati
  • 🏠 Didirisiwa tsa marulelo
  • 🎨 Dipente
  • 💡 Didirisiwa tsa motlakase
  • 🚰 Plumbing
  • 🪑 Ditulo
  • 🔊 Sound System
❤️ A nka sponsor selo sengwe fela?

Ee. O ka tlhopha sengwe se o batlang go se tshegetsa.

Sekao:

  • 🪟 Window
  • 🚪 Door
  • 🧱 Bricks
  • 🪑 Chairs
  • 💡 Lighting
  • 🔊 Sound System
  • 🏠 Roof
  • 🚰 Plumbing

Etela Didirisiwa kgotsa Donate page go bona tsotlhe tse di tlhokegang.

💳 Nka dira jang meneelo ya madi?

O ka dira meneelo ka ditsela tsotlhe tse kereke e di amogelang.

  • 🏦 Bank Transfer (EFT)
  • 📱 Electronic Payment
  • 💵 Meneelo ka kereke

Tshedimosetso yotlhe ya go duela e fitlhelwa mo go Donate page.

📄 A ke tla fiwa netefatso ya meneelo ya me?

Ee. Meneelo yotlhe e tla netefadiwa ke komiti ya kago morago ga gore e amogelwe le go tlhatlhobiwa.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 A malapa kgotsa dikhamphani di ka neela mmogo?

Ee. Re amogela meneelo go tswa go:

  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Malapa
  • 🏢 Dikhamphani
  • 🤝 Mekgatlho
  • ⛪ Diphuthego
  • ❤️ Batho ka bongwe
📊 Nka bona jang tswelopele ya porojeke?

Etela Progress page go bona:

  • 📈 Progress bar ya kago
  • 💰 Madi a a setse a kokoantswe
  • 🏗️ Dintšhafatso tsa kago
  • 🧱 Didirisiwa tse di setseng di tlhokega
📞 Nka ikgolaganya le mang fa ke na le dipotso?

Fa o na le dipotso mabapi le porojeke ya kago, tsweetswee etela Contact page kgotsa ikgolaganye le Building Committee ya Tshenolo The Holy Church.

❤️ Nka Thusa Jang Gape?

Le fa o sa kgone go neela madi, o sa ntse o ka tshegetsa porojeke ya kago.
