🏛 Goreng re Aga Kereke e Kgolo?

Kereke e Kgolo ga se moago fela. Ke boswa jo re bo agelang bana ba rona le dikokomana tsotlhe tse di tlang. Re aga lefelo la kobamelo, thuto, kutlwano le tirelo ya Tshenolo The Holy Church.



Lefelo la Kobamelo

Kereke e Kgolo e tla nna lefelo le badumedi botlhe ba kopanang mo go lone go obamela, go rapela le go direla Mme Morongwa le Badimo.

Lefelo la Thuto

Moagong ono go tla tshwarelwa dithuto tsa Bokonyana, Baebele, dipuisano le tsotlhe tse di nonotshang kitso ya badumedi.

Kopano ya Badumedi

Kereke e tla nna lefelo la kutlwano, lorato le bongwe jwa badumedi ba Tshenolo The Holy Church.

Dikopano tse Dikgolo

Go tla tshwarelwa dikopano tsa dikgaolo, dikopano tsa kereke le tsotlhe tsotlhe tse di botlhokwa mo Bokonyaneng.

Boswa jwa Bokonyana

Re aga boswa jo bo tla tswelelang mo melokong e e tlang le go tlotlomatsa dithuto tsa Tshenolo The Holy Church.

Bokamoso jwa Kereke

Moneelo mongwe le mongwe o aga bokamoso jwa Kereke le go thusa gore porojeke eno e felele ka katlego.

🌍 Ponelopele ya Rona

Re eletsa go bona Kereke e Kgolo e eme e le sesupo sa tumelo, kobamelo le kutlwano. E nne lefelo le meloko ya gompieno le ya ka moso e tla kopanang mo go lone go obamela, go ithuta le go aga Bokonyana.

🎯 Maikaelelo a Rona

Go aga Kereke e Kgolo ya Tshenolo The Holy Church.

Go kopanya badumedi botlhe mo kobamelong.

Go sireletsa boswa jwa Bokonyana.

Go godisa tumelo le kutlwano.

Go tlogelela bana le ditlogolo boswa jo bo sa khutleng.

Go aga lefelo le le tla direlang badumedi ba tsotlhe tsotlhe ka meloko.

🤝 O ka Tsenya Letsogo Jang?

Ntsha Moneelo

Moneelo mongwe le mongwe o thusa go aga Kereke e Kgolo.

Ntsha Didirisiwa

Neela ditena, samente, matlhabaphefo, mabati kgotsa didirisiwa tse dingwe tsa kago.

Rapela

Rapela gore porojeke eno e tswelele sentle ka kaelo ya Mme Morongwa le Badimo.

Tsaya Karolo

Tsaya karolo mo ditirong tsotlhe tse di amanang le kago ya Kereke.

Re Aga Boswa jwa Bokonyana

Setena sengwe le sengwe, moneelo mongwe le mongwe le thapelo nngwe le nngwe di aga Kereke ya rona. A Mme Morongwa le Badimo ba tswelele go segofatsa porojeke eno le mongwe le mongwe yo o tsenyang letsogo.
